Eternal Rewards and Inheritances

Free Ebook



The purpose of this Bible study ebook is to inform believers of the great rewards that accompany  a faithful, well-lived Christian life and the glory of the inheritance that is to come. To help them overcome worldliness, and to motivate them to Christian service and holiness.

These studies were  written for those who love to study Scripture and get into all the details. There are many cross-references which you can look up as you go along. Take your time. Feel free to teach them in your church, bible studies, home groups, cell groups and Bible colleges. There is no restriction on their use and they may be freely reproduced and distributed for non-profit ministry purposes.  There are some suggested discussion questions for each chapter at the back of this ebook.

You may contact the author, John Edmiston at johned@cybermissions.org

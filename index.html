Eternal Rewards and Inheritances

Free Ebook

Learn about the amazing rewards and inheritances that God has for faithful born-again, Bible-believing Christians! Discover what the Bible says about how you will receive a powerful resurrection body, a crown of righteousness, and be able to rule and reign with Christ. Learn how God’s servants are blessed for their sacrificial service and how overcomers are covered with glory in the heavenly realms!

The purpose of this Bible study ebook is to inform believers of the great rewards that accompany a faithful, well-lived Christian life and the glory of the inheritance that is to come. To help them overcome worldliness, and to motivate them to Christian service and holiness.

They were written as a response to the social and theological contexts created by the heretical Prosperity Gospel. They are also a response to liberal theology which scorns the whole idea of heavenly rewards and inheritances as philosophically naive. However, rewards and inheritances are clearly taught by Jesus and the apostles and prophets from Genesis to Revelation!

As we approach the time of the Great Tribulation many Christians are being persecuted all over the world especially in places such as Nigeria and Pakistan. Christians all over the world will need to understand their heavenly rewards and inheritance in order to remain steadfast and immovable in the Lord:

1 Corinthians 15:57–58
But thanks be to God, who gives us the victory through our Lord Jesus Christ. Therefore, my beloved brothers, be steadfast, immovable, always abounding in the work of the Lord, knowing that in the Lord your labor is not in vain.

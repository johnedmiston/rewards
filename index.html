Eternal Rewards and Inheritances

Free Bible Study Ebook

Learn about the amazing rewards and inheritances that God has for faithful born-again, Bible-believing Christians! Discover what the Bible says about how you will receive a powerful resurrection body, a crown of righteousness, and be able to rule and reign with Christ. Learn how God’s servants are blessed for their sacrificial service and how overcomers are covered with glory in the heavenly realms!

The purpose of this Bible study ebook is to inform believers of the great rewards that accompany a faithful, well-lived Christian life and the glory of the inheritance that is to come — to help them overcome worldliness and motivate them to Christian service and holiness.

These studies were written as a response to the social and theological contexts created by the heretical Prosperity Gospel and to liberal theology that scorns the idea of heavenly rewards and inheritances as philosophically naive. Yet rewards and inheritances are clearly taught by Jesus, the apostles, and the prophets from Genesis to Revelation.

As we approach the time of the Great Tribulation, many Christians are being persecuted throughout the world — especially in places such as Nigeria and Pakistan. Understanding our heavenly rewards and inheritance helps believers remain steadfast and immovable in the Lord:

1 Corinthians 15:57–58
But thanks be to God, who gives us the victory through our Lord Jesus Christ. Therefore, my beloved brothers, be steadfast, immovable, always abounding in the work of the Lord, knowing that in the Lord your labor is not in vain.
